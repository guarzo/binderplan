Pokemon Art Collection

A curated gallery treating Pokemon cards as art, not commodities

Welcome

This collection is curated as an art gallery, not an archive.

Cards are selected for aesthetic, emotional, and narrative impact. The binder represents my current taste, not everything I own. Restraint, replacement, and pacing are intentional design choices.

The binder is the present tense of your taste. The holding box is the past and the future.

Core Belief

Art first. Favorites second. Value is a bonus, not the goal.

Every card should either:

  • Strengthen the visual cohesion of the collection, or
  • Deepen attachment to a specific Pokemon
